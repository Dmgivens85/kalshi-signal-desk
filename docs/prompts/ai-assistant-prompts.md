# AI Assistant Prompt Guide

## AI Roles In This Product

- summarize relevant news and research
- classify relevance between external content and Kalshi markets
- enrich confidence and ranking inputs
- explain why a signal fired
- help draft human-readable morning digests

## Guardrails

- AI does not override deterministic risk controls.
- AI does not directly authorize execution.
- AI outputs should always be attached to evidence references and confidence metadata.

## Prompt Families

- relevance classification prompt
- overnight alert explanation prompt
- morning digest summarization prompt
- external-source contradiction prompt
- market movement interpretation prompt
